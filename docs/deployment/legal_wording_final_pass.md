# Legal Wording Final Pass

The platform must explicitly communicate its scope and limitations to avoid legal liabilities.

## Core Wording Principles

- **No Visa Guarantee**: The platform does not sponsor visas, nor does it guarantee visa sponsorship from employers.
- **No Job Guarantee**: The platform assists in finding relevant roles; it is not a recruitment agency guaranteeing placement.
- **No Relocation Guarantee**: Assistance with relocation is solely between the employer and candidate.
- **Recommendations are Informational**: Suggested jobs are algorithmic matches based on text parsing, provided "as-is".
- **Fit Score Role**: The fit score is a rule-based informational tool. It is **not** an employment decision, an automated rejection system, or an official ranking.
- **LLM Suggestions**: Any text generated by AI (if enabled) is assistive and requires human judgment. It does not replace professional advice.
- **Target Audience Clarity**: The platform's mission (France-first job intelligence for Tunisian IT candidates) is clearly stated.
- **Privacy Statement**: Clear terms explaining how CV data is parsed, stored locally, and potentially sent to OpenRouter (if enabled) strictly for feature extraction, and how users can delete their data at any time.

*Note: This is an MVP legal wording guideline for product copy. It does not replace formal Terms of Service or Privacy Policy drafted by legal counsel.*
